Pressure Frame Test Fixture

We provide custom test fixtures equipped with a mechanical pressure frame system, enabling testing in various configurations:

  • Single-face (top or bottom)
  • Double-face (top and bottom)
  • Side connections to the product

    • This simple and robust system is manually operated by the user.

    It is best suited for applications with moderate probe counts, due to the mechanical stress limits of this design.

    For more advanced applications, we also offer pneumatic interfaces equipped with light curtains and pneumatic cylinders, ensuring enhanced safety and operator comfort during test operations.
